## App name

Retro Vault Elite

## Short description

Track your retro game collection, values, wishlists, and market prices across classic consoles.

## Full description

Retro Vault Elite is a collector-first app for retro gaming fans who want one place to track what they own, what they want, and what their collection is worth.

Browse full console libraries across classic systems, mark games as owned or wanted, track what you paid, and compare your shelf against current market values. Every game includes real cover art, market pricing, and a detailed collector view built to make your collection feel premium.

With Retro Vault Elite you can:

- Track owned, wanted, and missing games
- View estimated loose and complete market values
- See your collection value in multiple currencies
- Open a detailed page for each game with pricing and collector notes
- Follow console completion progress
- Highlight favorites and top-shelf games
- Export your collection data

Retro Vault Elite is designed for collectors who care about the details, whether you are building a dream shelf, hunting bargains, or simply keeping your library organized.

Important notes:

- Market values are reference estimates and may change over time.
- Some cover art and pricing data come from linked third-party market sources.
- Banner advertising may be shown in mobile builds to help support continued development.
