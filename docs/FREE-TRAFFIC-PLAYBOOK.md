# Retro Vault Elite Free Traffic Playbook

This is the non-spam plan for getting real collectors to Retro Vault Elite without paying for ads.

## Goal

Bring in people who actually collect retro games, want to track their shelves, and might return often.

## Core rule

Do not mass-post the same link everywhere. Communities can flag the domain as spam. Post useful collector questions, ask for feedback, and only share the link where it fits the rules.

## Best first channels

- Facebook retro game collecting groups: ask for feedback from collectors and mention loose/CIB value tracking.
- Discord retro gaming servers: share the collector challenge and ask people to post their rank.
- Reddit communities that allow feedback or project posts: avoid pure link drops and answer every comment.
- Product Hunt: launch as a free retro game collection tracker.
- Show HN: frame it as a build story and ask for technical feedback.
- YouTube and TikTok creators: ask for honest feedback, not a paid promo.
- Retro forums and local game shop groups: share the tool as a collector resource.

## 7-day sprint

1. Post the Facebook feedback copy in one or two groups that allow app/tool discussion.
2. Share the collector challenge in Discord and ask people to reply with their collector rank.
3. Comment helpfully on five retro collecting posts, then share the tool only where it fits.
4. Message 10 small retro creators with the creator DM and ask for feedback.
5. Post a short video showing loose vs CIB tracking and put the challenge link in the caption.
6. Launch on Product Hunt or prepare the launch page and ask friends to comment honestly.
7. Check admin analytics, find the best source, and repeat the winning angle.

## Daily tracker

Track each post with:

- Date
- Channel
- Link used
- Views or reactions
- Comments
- Site visits
- New accounts
- Notes on what people asked for

## What not to do

- Do not post the same wording in ten places.
- Do not use fake accounts.
- Do not pretend to be an unrelated fan.
- Do not argue with moderators.
- Do not buy bot traffic.
- Do not send cold DMs that look automated.

## What to repeat

Repeat anything that gets comments from real collectors, even if traffic is small. Real replies are better than empty clicks.
