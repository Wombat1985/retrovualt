# Retro Vault Elite Market And Store Plan

## What is already working in the market

- GameEye is known for mobile-first collection tracking and wishlists.
- CLZ Games is strong on large catalog coverage, cloud sync, and barcode scanning.
- PriceCharting is strong on current market values and pricing trust.
- Cross-platform wrappers like Capacitor, Electron, and Tauri let one web product reach mobile and desktop stores.

## Product direction for Retro Vault Elite

- Fast shelf view with real cover art and instant owned versus missing recognition.
- Better collector intelligence: grails, market movers, completion by console, and value summaries.
- Barcode and camera-first add flow for use in stores and at flea markets.
- Cloud accounts and backups so collections sync across every device.
- Variant depth for region, cartridge-only, complete-in-box, sealed, manual, and grading notes.
- Marketplace hooks for buy, sell, trade, and watchlist alerts.

## Store rollout path

1. Web app plus PWA installability for immediate browser and Microsoft Store style packaging options.
2. Capacitor packaging for Android and iPhone builds.
3. Desktop packaging with Electron or Tauri for Windows and macOS distribution.
4. Shared backend for auth, sync, prices, artwork, and push notifications.

## External services to connect next

- PriceCharting for current price data.
- IGDB for broad metadata coverage and images where license terms fit the product.
- Cloud database and auth provider for user accounts.
- Analytics, crash reporting, and push notifications.
