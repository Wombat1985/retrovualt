Retro Vault Elite Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Retro Vault Elite helps users track retro video game collections, view market values, and manage ownership data. This policy explains what information the app may process when distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play.

Information stored on your device

The app stores collection preferences such as owned games, wanted games, favorite games, purchase prices, and currency choice on your device so your library can persist between sessions.

Market and catalog data

Game catalog records, price snapshots, and cover images are loaded from app content and linked market sources. Market values are reference estimates and may change over time.

Advertising

Retro Vault Elite may display a banner advertisement through Google AdMob. Ad providers may process device identifiers, coarse location, or other advertising data depending on the platform, your permissions, and your region.

Analytics and tracking

The current build does not include custom analytics. If iOS tracking authorization is requested, it is only for ad delivery and measurement through the advertising provider. Future versions may add analytics or account features, and this policy should be updated before release.

Sharing

Collection data is not sold by the app creator. Third-party advertising and app-platform providers may process limited data as required to deliver ads, downloads, crash handling, billing, or store compliance.

Your choices

  • You can reset collection data inside the app.
  • You can limit ad tracking through your device settings where supported.
  • You can choose not to submit purchase-price information.
