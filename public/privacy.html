Retro Vault Elite Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Retro Vault Elite helps users track retro video game collections, view market values, and manage ownership data. This policy explains what information the website and app may process.

Account information

If you create an account, Retro Vault Elite stores your email address, optional display name, password hash, account creation date, and collection sync data. Passwords are not stored in plain text.

Collection information

The app stores collection preferences such as owned games, wanted games, favorite games, complete-in-box marks, purchase prices, notes, barcode mappings, and currency choice so your library can persist between sessions.

Market and catalog data

Game catalog records, price snapshots, and cover images are loaded from app content and linked market sources. Market values are reference estimates and may change over time.

Advertising

Retro Vault Elite may display advertising through Google AdSense or Google AdMob. Ad providers may process identifiers, coarse location, or other advertising data depending on your platform, permissions, settings, and region.

Analytics and tracking

Retro Vault Elite uses Google Analytics and basic first-party page-view counting to understand traffic, popular pages, device type, and referrer sources. The first-party counter is used for aggregate product reporting and does not show public visitor lists.

Sharing

Collection data is not sold by the app creator. Third-party advertising, analytics, hosting, email, and app-platform providers may process limited data as required to deliver ads, analytics, password reset email, hosting, downloads, crash handling, billing, or store compliance.

Your choices

  • You can reset collection data inside the app.
  • You can delete your account inside account settings.
  • You can limit ad tracking through your device settings where supported.
  • You can choose not to submit purchase-price information.

Contact

Contact support through the Retro Vault Elite website: https://www.retrovaultelite.com/support.html.
