What makes a retro games tracker worth using?

A real retro game collection tracker should understand how collectors think. It needs full console libraries, region support, condition tracking, loose and complete values, wanted games, paid prices, favorites, and progress toward completing a set. Retro Vault Elite is built around those collector habits so you can see what you own, what it may be worth, and what you should hunt next.
