About Retro Vault Elite

A serious retro game collection tracker for people who love the hunt.

Retro Vault Elite helps collectors catalogue games across classic console libraries, track loose and complete-in-box ownership, follow wishlist targets, understand collection value, and spot the next game worth chasing.

Open the app Explore retro game tools

Built for collectors

Track owned, wanted, missing, favourite, loose, complete, sealed, graded, paid price, and account-synced collection data without turning your hobby into a spreadsheet.

Global libraries

Browse retro console libraries across North America, PAL regions, Japan, Asia-English releases, handhelds, classic computers, and arcade-style collector systems.

Value with context

Separate loose from complete-in-box value, compare paid prices with tracked value, and use market snapshots as a collector guide rather than a guaranteed sale price.

Why this exists

Retro collecting is part archive, part treasure hunt, and part personal museum. Retro Vault Elite is designed to make that feel exciting: what you own, what you are missing, what your strongest console is, which grails are still out there, and how your shelf is growing over time.
